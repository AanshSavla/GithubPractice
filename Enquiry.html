MILAN STORE

History:

Milan Store was set up in Byculla in Mumbai at 1996.It was the idea of Lalji Jagshi Savla and his son Jitendra Lalji Savla.The store was famous for its Ladies as well as Gents Wear.After 2008 it stopped the ladies wear and fully concentrated on Men's Wear.Today Milan Store is one of the famous cloth store in Byculla for its exclusive Men's Wear.It is continuously upgrading its stocks and trying to bring new styles and variety in fabrics of shirt ,pant ,coat ,kurta etc.
